Helper tool to create offset of nordic ski route relations on Opensnowmap rendering

Instructions

Adjust the pistes lateral offset from the list below. When you are pleased with the result, press 'export'.
The resulting file can be sent to webmaster_at_opensnowmap.org or committed to github. Simply fork, add your file, commit and send a pull request.
Your work will be taken into account in the map rendering.
